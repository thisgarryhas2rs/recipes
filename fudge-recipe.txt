Ingredients:
 - 3 cups chocolate chips
 - 1 can sweetned confensed milk
 - 1/4 cup butter

Directions:
  1. Put all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Microwave for 2 minutes and stir.
  3. Microwave for another 2 minutes and stir.
  4. Pour mixture into well-greased 8x8 glass baking dish. Refrigerate until firm.
